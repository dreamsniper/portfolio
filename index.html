A Little About Me

"Steven Taveras is a New York City artist and student seriously dedicated to Frontend Developement at BMCC. He has a background in fine arts and has a degree in Visual Communications. He was a freelance graphic designer before starting work with a non-profit company dealing with Gems and Gemology and obtaining his Accredited Jewelry Professional License.

When Steven isn't bleary eyed from working and studying on the computer he enjoys casual gaming, reading fantasy and science fiction novels. Brunch and nature hikes with friends, and quiet time grooming his two siamese cats Sookie and Goddrick (named after True Blood characters).
